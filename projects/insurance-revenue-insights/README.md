# Insurance Analytics — Revenue Insights

## Overview

An insurance revenue report designed to highlight revenue performance and enable investigation of the policies behind the results. A dedicated drill-through page connects summary indicators to policy-level detail.

> Screenshots, exact KPI definitions, findings, and business outcomes are pending validation against the report.

## Report pages

1. **Revenue Insights** — primary revenue-performance analysis
2. **Policy Details Drillthrough** — detailed view of a selected policy
3. **Info** — report guidance and supporting information

## Business questions

- How is revenue performing overall and across key segments?
- Which segments or policies contribute most to revenue?
- Where are revenue weaknesses or opportunities emerging?
- What policy-level detail explains a summary result?

## Capabilities demonstrated

- Revenue KPI design and segmentation
- Summary-to-policy analytical flow
- Context-aware policy drill-through
- Insurance-domain dashboard storytelling
